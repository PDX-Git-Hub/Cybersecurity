# Recover (RC)

**Purpose:**  
Restore systems and improve resilience after a cybersecurity incident.

## Key Practices
- Perform regular data backups and test restoration
- Develop disaster recovery and business continuity plans
- Restore affected systems and services quickly
- Conduct post-incident lessons learned reviews
- Update security controls based on incident findings

## Example Controls
- Offline and secure backups
- Recovery Time Objectives (RTO) documentation
- Post-incident reports and improvement plans
- Updates to policies and security tools after incidents

## Why It Matters
Recovery ensures business continuity, minimizes downtime, and strengthens defenses to prevent future incidents.
