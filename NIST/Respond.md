# Respond (RS)

**Purpose:**  
Take action regarding a detected cybersecurity incident.

## Key Practices
- Develop and follow an incident response plan
- Define communication channels for incidents
- Contain affected systems to limit damage
- Document all incident actions and findings
- Perform root cause analysis

## Example Controls
- Incident response playbooks
- Isolation procedures for compromised devices
- Incident reporting templates and logs
- Coordination with IT and security teams

## Why It Matters
A structured response reduces confusion, limits damage, and ensures compliance with legal and regulatory requirements.
